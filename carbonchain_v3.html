ℹ️
How This Prevents Double Counting
In traditional systems, a registry could list the same credit multiple times across different databases. Here, every tonne of CO₂ offset is a unique ERC-1155 token with a serial number. The blockchain enforces single ownership — no wallet can receive a token that is already owned by another wallet. When a buyer retires a credit to claim their offset, the token is permanently burned (destroyed). A burned token can never be transferred or sold again.
